## Research Interests

<div class="research-grid" markdown="1">

### Wireless Communications
Optimization techniques for next-generation wireless systems, including massive MIMO, cell-free networks, and NOMA.

### Machine Learning
Deep learning and reinforcement learning applications for intelligent wireless network optimization and resource allocation.

### Signal Processing
Signal processing methods for communication systems, including channel estimation and interference management.

### Federated Learning
Energy-efficient federated learning frameworks for distributed wireless networks and UAV communications.
